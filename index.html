Welcome to the URAWarrior community!

You're not alone because we're in this together!!

Hey, Warrior. There’s no reason to face your struggles alone. We see you — you’re looking for something bigger. Our community is built for you! Welcome to your first step toward a life of peace with the help of the revolutionary URAWARRIOR mental wellness app and community. This is a safe and inclusive space where no one is alone. Here, you matter. Bullying, trolling, negativity, and bigotry are never tolerated. Inspiration, encouragement and empowerment are EVERYTHING!

Founded by a Black woman and created by a diverse team, URAWARRIOR celebrates differences and embraces all walks of life. You are invited to join us on this journey as we remove the stigma around mental illness, heal and live our lives to the fullest!

What makes us different?

Find a community

Be uplifted, inspired, and empowered by the community that surrounds you. Share, learn, and grow on your journey to becoming your best, healthiest self. It takes 21 days to create a regular practice and 90 days to make that practice part of your everyday lifestyle. Your path starts here.

Warrior Challenge

Join a Warrior Challenge to take daily steps to self-improvement with our 14-day, 21-day, and 90-day Challenges. It takes 21 days to develop a new habit and 90 days to make it a lifestyle.

Share your story

Share your story with us on the warrior wall or during the warrior challenge. The first step to healing is sharing your authentic warrior story.

The URAWarrior Method

We partnered with coaches, therapists, and doctors to develop the URAWarrior Method. The four-step method includes four pillars:

  • Personal Development
  • Self-Improvement
  • Motivation
  • Support
